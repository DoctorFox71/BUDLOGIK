About us

Budlogik was founded in 2021 with the aim of providing high quality construction works and design services

In the process of the company's development in the conditions of more complicated supply chains and slowdown of export-import operations, our experts designed and developed activities in wholesale of mineral fertilizers, agricultural products, polyethylene films, auto and motor transport, motorcycle equipment.

Despite the relative youth of the company, our partners treat us as a mature organization due to well-established business processes, clear structure, individual approach, assignment of only one key-account-manager to one client and unconditional fulfillment of obligations and guarantees.

We are a fast-growing company specializing in construction and trade. We promote our experience, synergy of innovations and proven methods, build long-term relationships with customers. We create significant value for our customers, are always open to new ideas and welcome new partners.

About product

Today, our products are the result of the joint work of specialized European research centers and professionals, and our technologies are presented in more than 15 countries (we carefully select, train and cooperate only with proven distributors and partners).

Our products are unique, as they are created taking into account climatic conditions, soil and crops on which they will be used.

Monitoring the quality of our fertilizers in our in-house research and development center, as well as communicating with distributors and farmers, enables us to respond quickly to changes and market needs, and allows farmers and partners to achieve better yields, profits, save time and reduce environmental impact.

We guarantee high quality products, because the production is carried out on modern high-tech equipment using only the highest quality raw materials. This allows us not only to reduce production costs and environmental impact, but also to avoid phytotoxicity for plants.
